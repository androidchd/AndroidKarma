The soul of the Lakelands, Klub Karma, is equipped with facilities like tennis and squash courts, separate splash pool for children, heated indoor lap-pool, spa, library lounge, party terraces, coffee shop, fine dining restaurants, gymnasium, patisserie and Isha - the yoga & meditation room.

Klub Karma will also sport a banquet hall and an additional terrace garden, with the option of a golf side garden setting to celebrate special occasions like weddings, parties, luxury launches and more.
