In our pursuit of building a bright and sustainable future, Karma Lakelands presents the Day Care Centre. Run by the NGO Sanskriti Saurabh, it is home to around 70 children where their education, clothing and food is looked after by committed staff & volunteers.

While our gracious residents, golfers and visitors often sponsor meals and the education of a number of children, our organic garden and sustainable farming products will directly contribute to their welfare.

If you wish to support this endeavour or take a tour of the facilities, please do contact the staff at the site.
